About Us

At GymSupps, we're more than just a supplement store — we're your dedicated fitness partner. Founded with a passion for health, performance, and community, our mission is to help you fuel your goals with top-quality supplements you can trust. Whether you're a seasoned athlete, a gym newcomer, or someone working on building a healthier lifestyle, we offer carefully selected products to support strength, endurance, recovery, and overall well-being. Our knowledgeable team is here to guide you with honest advice, tailored recommendations, and a commitment to helping you achieve your best self — inside and outside the gym.

Our Story

Founded by fitness enthusiasts in the heart of Beirut, GymSupps started with a simple mission: to make high-quality, effective supplements accessible to everyone — from beginners to elite athletes.

It all began when Raphael Rizk, a personal trainer turned entrepreneur, noticed how hard it was to find trusted supplements locally. Either the quality was inconsistent or the prices were unreasonable. So, he decided to change that.

What started as a small online shop run from a home office quickly grew into a go-to destination for serious lifters, health-conscious individuals, and gym owners across Lebanon and beyond. Every product we offer is hand-picked, tested, and backed by science — because your health and performance deserve the best.

At GymSupps, we're more than just a store — we're a community.
